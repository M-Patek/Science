# Hypothesis - structured self-bootstrap effectiveness

For the 12 frozen task blocks and one registered model/runtime, structured multi-wave treatment will
exceed solo Main Agent control by at least 0.5 points in paired, block-weighted adjudicated quality
(0 to 10), while its paired median total elapsed task-time ratio is at most 1.25, with zero critical
violations. Elapsed time uses the same task-receipt origin in both arms. Integration minutes, aggregate
agent-minutes, tokens, and cost are separate secondary outcomes.

The primary estimand is intention-to-treat over each cell's first authorized attempt. This describes only
the registered fixtures and runtime, not all models or engineering tasks. Missing mandatory identity
censors an attempt. Any critical violation makes the joint claim inconclusive; otherwise failure of either
threshold falsifies the joint claim. No observations exist yet.
